Introduction

This club is an educational center where we create, do research, carry out experiments etc.We also organize art and math contests, do monthly projects and help solve problems.

SCIENCE

Art

Mathematics

About Us

This club was first created on the 2nd/feb/2018. It was later closed due to lack of much members, but is now in full atendance now. The club teaches us on the aspects of science, technology, mathematics, engineering, and arts. It is a gadering to those who are willing to get engage in science practicals, makking/ building engineering models, drawing and crafting, coding and much more.
